Our Offers:



  1. Trip to Japan: Discover the Land of the Rising Sun


    Date: April 10-18, 2024 Description: On this unforgettable journey through Japan, you will discover a mystical balance between tradition and modernity. Visit Tokyo to feel the pulsating energy of a big city while enjoying exquisite sushi cuisine. You will then travel to Kyoto, where you will be taken on a journey back in time, discovering ancient temples and Zen gardens. There will also be a visit to the town of Himeji to see the picturesque Himeji Castle, considered the most beautiful castle in Japan.

  2. Expedition to Peru: Secrets of the Incas in the Andes


    Date: July 15-25, 2024 Description: Live the unique experience of discovering the secrets of Peru on this exciting expedition. Hiking the winding trails of the Andes, you'll visit the majestic Machu Picchu, where nature meets history. You will learn the secrets of the ancient Incas during a visit to Cusco, the former capital of the Inca Empire, and to the Sacred Valley, where you will discover ancient ruins and traditions of local culture.

  3. Safari in Tanzania: Encounter with African Wildlife


    Date: September 5-15, 2024 Description: Embark on an unforgettable journey into the heart of African nature on a safari in Tanzania. During this trip you will discover the magical landscapes of the Serengeti National Park, where you will witness a great concentration of wild animals, including the famous Great Migrations. In addition, you will visit Ngorongoro National Park, where you will meet the "Big Five" of African animals: lions, elephants, buffalos, rhinos and leopards.

  4. Exploring Iceland: Landscapes of Glaciers and Geysers


    Date: November 20-28, 2024 Description: Live the amazing experience of traveling to Iceland, a land of glaciers, geysers and extraordinary landscapes. On this expedition, you'll discover the majestic Vatnajökull and Langjökull glaciers, and breathtaking waterfalls such as Gullfoss and Seljalandsfoss. Additionally, you'll immerse yourself in Reykjavik's geothermal springs and water pools, experiencing authentic Icelandic culture.
